Our Story

Osprey Security (OSEC) started as Association for Information Technology Professionals (AITP). Since then we have grown dramatically, we are a group of highly motivated, aspiring technology professionals, looking to improve everyday. We compete in national competitions include CCDC and CPTC, in Spring 2023 our CCDC made school history by reaching nationals. We are committed to helping UNF students from any background or major gain experience in IT. Go Ospreys!
